Methodology & data sources

Social. Owned-audience follower counts from the official TikTok, Instagram and Facebook profiles (dated snapshots, manually updated). Public TikTok hashtag view counts are no longer exposed; #buildabear scale is tracked via TikTok Creative Center (roadmap).

Community. Subscriber history reconstructed from Wayback Machine snapshots of old.reddit.com (run scripts/backfill_reddit_wayback.py from an open network); the Dec-2023 point (27,351) is verified. Conversation taxonomy is illustrative pending a classification pass.

Search. Google Trends, U.S., monthly, normalized 0–100; manual CSV export ingested via scripts/ingest_google_trends.py. A 12-month moving average isolates trend from seasonality.

Digital. Cumulative visits for "Build-A-Bear Tycoon" (universe 4107870885) pulled live from the Roblox public games API via scripts/fetch_roblox.py.

Online demand. E-commerce demand growth by quarter as reported in BBW FY2025 results.

Resale. Curated, documented eBay sold prices (realized, not asking); ongoing comps ingested via scripts/ingest_resale.py.

Retail & commercial. Location counts and full-year segment revenue from BBW SEC filings & press releases, FY2024–FY2025.
