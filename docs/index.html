Digital engagement — Roblox

live anchor

What it is: "Build-A-Bear Tycoon" is an official Roblox game built by Gamefam (a top Roblox studio) in partnership with BBW, launched Dec 2022. A "tycoon" is a build-and-manage game — here players run their own virtual Build-A-Bear workshop, which keeps the brand and its products in front of a teen/tween audience for hours at a time. What 24M means: cumulative visits — the total number of times players have entered the experience since launch — pulled live from the Roblox API. It's an independent, hard-to-game engagement signal (note: cumulative visits only ever rise; what matters is the pace of new visits).

06

Online demand — e-commerce

SEC filings

Two things are true at once, and both matter. Who shops online is decisively adult — the buildabear.com audience skews 25–34 and female. But the volume of online order demand has softened: after a record Q2 FY2025 it turned negative for three straight quarters and fell 26.1% in Q1 FY2026, with management citing weaker consumer traffic. So the online channel is structurally the adult channel — but its recent demand trend is a real risk to watch, not a tailwind.
