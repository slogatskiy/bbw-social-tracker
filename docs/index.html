The setup, in numbers

High brand recognition, a large loyalty base, and a customer mix that is already ~40% teens & adults. The question is whether the adult demand is accelerating — that is what the signals below test.

Culture & TikTok

05

Methodology & data sources

Search interest. Google Trends, U.S., monthly, normalized 0–100. Pulled by manual CSV export (Google blocks automated access) and ingested via scripts/ingest_google_trends.py.

Reddit community. Historical subscriber counts reconstructed from Wayback Machine snapshots of old.reddit.com; current counts via the Reddit API. The Dec-2023 anchor (27,351) is verified.

Culture. Qualitative evidence from TikTok, Entrepreneur, and Suzy coverage of the adult-collector trend. View-count tracking is the next signal to be automated.

Financial anchors. Customer mix, loyalty base, awareness, and revenue from BBW SEC filings (8-K) and BBW investor presentations (2024).

Status. KPI cards use real, cited figures. Time-series charts marked illustrative are seeded for design and are overwritten with live data when the collector scripts are run.

Why convergence. Any one platform can spike on a fad. Independent sources moving together is what distinguishes a durable fandom from noise.
